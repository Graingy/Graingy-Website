Subsidiaries


Graingy has historically had few subsidiaries, preferring to keep operations internal, but there have been a small number nonetheless.


Operational



Sandude Airlines MSMRJ-220-02


Sandude Airlines


Created as Graingy's civilian air service, the company's domain has been expanded and shrunken over the years, though remains in operation regardless. Currently Graingy itself handles most air freight and high capacity air transport, however Sandude keeps its role in providing shorter range flights from smaller airfields.

GraNet logo


GraNet


Originally founded as a department of Graingy dedicated to researching computer networking in the late 1950s, GraNet became an independent subsidiary in 1972 to continue its work on a broader scope. It was reincorporated into the company in 1995 and once again operates as a department of Graingy, managing and maintaining its vast servers and databanks, along with the infrastructure running between them.
